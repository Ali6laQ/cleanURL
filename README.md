# ✨ CleanURL

Hey there! 👋 Welcome to **CleanURL**.

Have you ever copied a link from TikTok or Instagram to share with a friend, only to realize it's super long and full of random letters and numbers? Those extra characters are usually just trackers used by apps. 

**CleanURL** is a simple, fast, and secure web tool that automatically removes all that unnecessary tracking junk from your links. You get a nice, clean, and short link that is much better and safer to share! 🚀

## 🌟 What makes it cool?

* **🪄 Auto-Paste Magic:** Just click the input box! If you already have a TikTok or Instagram link copied to your clipboard, the tool will automatically paste it for you. 
* **✂️ Goodbye Trackers:** It instantly takes your messy link and cuts off the tracking parameters (everything after the `?` symbol).
* **🕵️‍♂️ Smart TikTok Unshortener:** If you paste a shortened TikTok link (like `vt.tiktok.com` or `vm.tiktok.com`), the tool is smart enough to find the real, full link first, and *then* cleans it up for you!
* **📤 Easy Copy & Share:** Once your link is beautifully clean, a pop-up appears. You can copy the new link with one click (complete with a satisfying "Copied!" animation) or share it directly using your device's native sharing menu.

## 🛠️ How to use it?

It is incredibly beginner-friendly! You don't need any technical skills:

1. **Copy** any video link from the Instagram or TikTok app. 📱
2. **Open** the CleanURL website and click inside the text box. 🖱️
3. **Click** the colorful **"نظف الرابط" (Clean Link)** button. ✨
4. **Boom!** 🎉 Your clean link is ready. Hit copy or share! 

## 💻 What's under the hood?

This project is lightweight and built with simple web technologies:
* **HTML:** To build the structure of the page.
* **Tailwind CSS:** To make the design look modern, beautiful, and responsive on all screens. 🎨
* **Vanilla JavaScript:** To handle all the logic, clipboard reading, and cleaning operations behind the scenes. ⚙️
